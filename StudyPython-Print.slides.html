$$ \hat{\sigma} = \frac{\sum (x_i - \bar{x})^2}{n -1} $$

$$ \sigma(\bar{x}) = \frac{\sigma}{\sqrt{n}} $$ $$ \sigma(\bar{x}) = \frac{\hat{\sigma}}{\sqrt{n}} $$
